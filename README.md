# AI Governance Framework

Practical AI governance templates, checklists, and frameworks for responsible AI deployment aligned to the EU AI Act and global standards.

Built by TrustGrid Technology Limited.

## Why AI Governance Matters

Organisations are deploying AI tools faster than their governance frameworks can keep up. A customer service chatbot starts answering FAQs and ends up influencing loan decisions. A hiring tool trained on historical data quietly filters out candidates. A fraud detection model flags transactions in ways nobody on the compliance team can explain.

AI governance is not about slowing down innovation. It is about knowing what you have deployed, understanding what decisions it is making, and being able to demonstrate that those decisions are fair, accountable, and compliant with applicable regulations.

## EU AI Act Risk Levels

| Risk Level | Description | Examples |
|---|---|---|
| Unacceptable Risk | Prohibited AI practices | Social scoring, real-time biometric surveillance |
| High Risk | Regulated AI requiring conformity assessment | Credit scoring, recruitment tools, medical devices |
| Limited Risk | Transparency obligations only | Chatbots, deepfakes |
| Minimal Risk | No specific obligations | Spam filters, AI in video games |
